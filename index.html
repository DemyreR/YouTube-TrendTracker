About The Site

This website is a valuable resource for YouTube content creators, providing assistance in navigating the dynamic landscape of daily trends. Our platform gathers daily data and statistics directly from YouTube, loading information from the top 200 videos into a comprehensive database to then be analyzed. Whether you're a seasoned creator or just starting, our platform offers insights into the most utilized tags, helping you optimize your content strategy. Stay informed about the current top 10 videos, explore the spread of various video types, track daily changes, view tomorrow's predictions, and discover popular tags that resonate with your audience.

How To Use The Site

This website is designed with user-friendliness in mind, ensuring a seamless experience for content creators. To get started, simply navigate through the intuitive menu located at the top of the page. The "Home" link takes you to this page, providing a quick overview of the site's features. Explore "Current Top 10" to discover currently trending videos, "Current Trends" for insights into popular video types, "Daily Change" to stay informed about daily shifts in trending video types, and "Predicted Change" to see the predicted shifts in said types. Additionally, use "Popular Tags" to identify tags that a currently resonating with audiences. Enjoy the ease of use and make the most of the valuable insights provided to elevate your YouTube content.
